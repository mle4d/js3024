Our Story PDX Kids Code Club started out slowly & organically in 2016 after my son showed a few friends a website game we had built together. These friends also wanted to learn how to make their sites & games & soon enough it turned into weekly planned after school mettings where I'd help the kids build up their own projects little by little, learning what they needed to learn as new ideas came to them. After a few months, my son's little group of friends had snow balled into three dozen kids from his school, across all grades in their elementary school who had heard about what they had been doing one by one. Fast-forward to now & we have five chapters throughout Portland, over 400 members & hundreds of volunteers making this club happen.
