Help Out! If you would like to help out PDX Kids Code Club, we'd love that & there's many ways to do so! Our Club heavily relies on the support & genorisity of our great city, subcommunities, sponsors, donors & awesome volunteers. Please use the email form for any questions or to get started on volunteering, donating, or becoming a sponsor.

Ways You Can Support

  1. Donations- Donations help our group continue on so we can pay fees for meet-up venues, fund hardware projects & spare equipment for members who don't have a personal laptop. If you have items to donate, that is great too! Please include the type of item in the email using the form on this page.

  2. Sponsorships- PDX Kids Code Club tries to keep tuition amounts as low as possible but we know it's still too high for our group to be accessible to all kids. With sponsoring, you can provide a whole years tuition to children in our group who wouldn't be able to join otherwise. We have Three different amounts for sponsorship:
    • Turquoise tuition sponsorship is $200 a year for one member
    • Amethyst tuition sponsorship is $300 a year for one member
    • Summer Camp To sponsor a member to go to our annual summer day camp for a week in July, the cost is $350

  3. Volunteering- We are always in need of volunteers! Volunteers do not need to have any experience with coding or even with tech! Volunteers need to be at least 16 years old of age. Volunteers 18 & over must pass a background check. If there is a specific chapter you'd like to volunteer with, please specify which one(s) in your email to us.
