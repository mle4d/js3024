Want to Join? If your child would like to join, please pick any Chapter of your choice, whether that be based off of location or what day of the week works for your schedule, & see information below on the two sub-groups of each chapter for their age range & tuition prices. Once you have decided on a chapter, feel free to drop in & observe a meet-up with your child & you can sign them up in person towards the end of the meet-up. If you'd prefer to sign your child up via email, feel free to fill out the email form below & we will get that started. Tuition is due at the first meet-up of every month for that month. Unfortunatly we cannot give partial refunds if your child misses a weekly meet-up after tuition has been paid but you can stop attending/paying for full months & start back up at any time. If your financial situation makes paying tuition difficult, please reach out to our team via the email form below & make note of your situation. We may have an open sponsored tuition spot available based on what Chapter(s) work for you & your family income.
Turquoise Group

Ages: 5-10 years old
Tution: $20 a month

Two hours once a week
& one monthly weekend club-wide meet-up

Our Turquoise group is for the younger half of our club. We try to have a 4:1 member to volunteer ratio so that those who need help have it no matter what. Our older Amethyst group is also around during Turquoise's meet-ups & often times will jump in to help & mentor when they can. Members of the Turquoise group work on one small solo project a month & one group project by the end of each month.
Amethyst Group

Ages: 11-15 years old
Tution: $30 a month

Three hours once a week
& one monthly weekend club-wide meet-up

Our Amethyst group is for our older half of the group. We try to have a 6:1 member/volunteer ratio. Each member of the Amythst group works on two personal projects & one group (3-5 members per group) project a month. Our Amythyst members are also welcomed to be volunteers at other Chapter meet-ups, volunteering for six meet-ups (18 hours) a month pays your tuition for the following month.
