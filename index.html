Welcome! Thanks for visiting the PDX Kids Code Club site! If you are a child or have a child interested in joining, check out the Chapters page of our site & pick any meet-up to stop in & check it out accompanied with a parent or guardian! Pamphlet information on joining & meet-up crew for any questions is available at all club meet-ups. We currently have five chapters through out the city of Portland, avaiable to all kids ages 5-15! Volunteer positions to help (& learn!) for ages 16 & beyond are always open! If you have any questions, main contact information for the club is available here or by chapter on the chapter page of the site.
